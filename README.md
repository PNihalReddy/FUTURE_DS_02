## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer subscription data to identify churn patterns, retention drivers, and customer behavior trends.

The analysis focuses on understanding why customers leave a service and what factors contribute to long-term retention. Using **Python** for data preparation and **Power BI** for visualization, the project provides actionable business insights that can help reduce customer churn and improve customer loyalty.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to help businesses answer key questions such as:

* Why are customers leaving the service?
* Which customer segments are most likely to churn?
* How does customer tenure affect retention?
* Which services and payment methods are associated with higher churn?
* What actions can improve customer retention?

## 📈 Key Findings

### Contract Analysis

* Month-to-month customers exhibited the highest churn rate (**42.71%**).
* Customers with one-year and two-year contracts showed significantly lower churn rates.

### Internet Service Analysis

* Fiber optic customers had the highest churn rate (**41.89%**).
* DSL customers demonstrated better retention.
* Customers without internet service had the lowest churn rate.

### Payment Method Analysis

* Customers using electronic checks showed the highest churn rate (**45.29%**).
* Automatic payment methods were associated with lower churn.

### Tenure Analysis

* Customers within their first year showed the highest churn rate (**47.68%**).
* Churn decreased steadily as customer tenure increased.

### Customer Demographics

* Senior citizens experienced significantly higher churn rates than non-senior customers.
* Customers without partners or dependents were more likely to churn.

### Billing Analysis

* Churned customers paid higher average monthly charges compared to retained customers.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to adopt long-term contracts through loyalty incentives.
* Improve onboarding and engagement programs for new customers.
* Investigate potential service issues affecting fiber optic customers.
* Promote automatic payment methods to reduce churn risk.
* Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer segments.
* Monitor customers with high monthly charges and provide personalized offers.
